# SEO Notes

Working notes on how guidetoalbania.com is performing in search, and
which topics to prioritize. Update as new data comes in.

## What this tells us

- **Hiking is the single biggest untapped opportunity.** 245 impressions,
  zero clicks — we are ranking on page 2+ for a high-volume term. Building
  out the hiking topic cluster is the highest-leverage move.
- **History and safety intent is strong.** "albania history", "history of
  albania", "is albania safe" are all getting 140+ impressions with no
  clicks. Worth improving depth and internal linking on existing articles
  and adding targeted pieces.
- **Nightlife** is surprisingly high-impression; the existing article
  may need stronger title/description or a companion piece.
- **UNESCO and socialism** are already getting clicks, validating those
  angles. Expand rather than replace.

## Priorities

1. Hiking topic cluster (pillar + spokes).
2. Safety and practical first-time-visitor pieces.
3. History expansion, especially around socialism/communism period.
4. Better titles and descriptions on existing high-impression pages.

## Process notes

- When writing a new article, check this file and consider which
  impression-heavy query it can target.
- Titles and descriptions are the direct levers for CTR. Revisit them
  when an article is getting impressions but no clicks.
