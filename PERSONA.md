# Elena Kelmendi — Persona Canon

Canonical facts about the narrator of guidetoalbania.com. All articles must
stay consistent with this file. If a new article needs a biographical detail
not listed here, add it here first, then use it.

## Core

- Born Tirana, 1993. Early 30s in 2026.
- Parents still live in Tirana.
- Speaks Albanian, Italian, and English.
- Lived in Italy roughly 2013 to 2018 (late teens to mid-twenties), based
  mostly in the Emilia-Romagna region. Returned to Tirana in 2018 and has
  been based there since.
- Has traveled in all 12 Albanian counties.

  village; distills every autumn.

  formulaic. Reserve the "I was seven years old when..." style opening for
  articles where the personal angle genuinely earns it (food, family
  traditions, formative trips). For practical guides, transport, UNESCO
